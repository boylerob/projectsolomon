	•	Definition: Scripture in its original manuscripts is without error in all that it affirms. Rooted in God's character and affirmed by Christ’s use of Scripture.

	•	Synonyms: supremacy, binding truth, divine standard

	•	Definition: God’s initiative to draw people into relationship and mission. Includes general (salvation) and specific (service) callings (Romans 8:28–30).

219. Commission
	•	Synonyms: mandate, sending, task, charge
	•	Lemmas: apostellō (G649), ṣāwāh (H6680)
	•	Definition: God’s entrusting of His people with gospel proclamation and kingdom work. Most clearly expressed in the Great Commission (Matthew 28:18–20).

220. Ministry
	•	Synonyms: service, work, vocation, calling
	•	Lemmas: diakonia (G1248), šārēt (H8334)
	•	Definition: Any Spirit-empowered act of service for the good of others and the glory of God. Includes preaching, mercy, leadership, and more (Ephesians 4:11–13).

✅ Terms 226–250: Authority, Mission, Kingdom, Identity & Destiny

226. Spiritual Authority

	•	Lemmas: exousia (G1849), mimshāl (H4475)

227. Apostleship
	•	Synonyms: sent one, delegate, foundational leader
	•	Lemmas: apostolos (G652)
	•	Definition: A specific calling to be sent with Christ’s authority—especially the Twelve and Paul. Broader application in missionary and church-planting roles (Ephesians 4:11).

228. Prophecy
	•	Synonyms: divine speech, proclamation, foretelling, exhortation
	•	Lemmas: prophēteia (G4394), nābîʾ (H5030)
	•	Definition: Speaking on behalf of God by the Spirit. May involve future insight, but primarily aimed at exhortation and revelation (1 Corinthians 14:3; Amos 3:7).

229. Teaching
	•	Synonyms: instruction, explanation, guidance, exegesis
	•	Lemmas: didaskalia (G1319), tôrāh (H8451)
	•	Definition: Communicating truth clearly and faithfully. Central to discipleship and doctrinal formation (Matthew 28:20; 2 Timothy 2:2).

230. Discipleship (Expanded)
	•	Synonyms: spiritual growth, following, obedience, apprenticeship
	•	Lemmas: mathētēs (G3101)
	•	Definition: Ongoing transformation into Christlikeness through relational learning, obedience, and mission (Luke 14:27; Colossians 1:28).

231. Kingdom of God
	•	Synonyms: reign, rule, dominion, divine order
	•	Lemmas: basileia tou theou (G932 + G2316)
	•	Definition: The dynamic, redemptive reign of God—present in Christ, expanding through the Church, fulfilled in eternity (Mark 1:15; Luke 17:21).

232. Gospel of the Kingdom
	•	Synonyms: good news, proclamation, message of reign
	•	Lemmas: euangelion (G2098) + basileia (G932)
	•	Definition: The good news that God reigns through Christ—bringing salvation, justice, and new creation (Matthew 24:14; Luke 4:43).

233. Covenant People
	•	Synonyms: chosen people, family of God, holy nation
	•	Lemmas: laos theou (G2992 + G2316), ʿam bĕrit (H5971 + H1285)
	•	Definition: Those bound to God through His promises—first Israel, then the Church through Christ. Identity rooted in grace (1 Peter 2:9; Genesis 17).

234. Body of Christ (Expanded)
	•	Synonyms: spiritual organism, Church, unified believers
	•	Lemmas: sōma Christou (G4983 + G5547)
	•	Definition: The collective, Spirit-empowered community of believers, with Christ as the Head. Expresses unity, diversity, and mission (1 Corinthians 12).

235. Bride of Christ (Expanded)
	•	Synonyms: beloved, covenant partner, redeemed people
	•	Lemmas: nymphē (G3565)
	•	Definition: A metaphor for the Church’s love and loyalty to Christ. Points to future consummation and purity (Revelation 19:7; Ephesians 5:25–27).

236. Priesthood of Believers
	•	Synonyms: access to God, spiritual ministry, intercessors
	•	Lemmas: hiereus (G2409), kōhēn (H3548)
	•	Definition: All believers are called to offer spiritual sacrifices and intercede. Direct access to God through Christ (1 Peter 2:5; Hebrews 10:19–22).

237. Royal Inheritance
	•	Synonyms: reward, possession, promise, future glory
	•	Lemmas: klēronomia (G2817), naḥălâ (H5159)
	•	Definition: What believers receive as co-heirs with Christ—eternal life, resurrection, kingdom authority (Romans 8:17; 1 Peter 1:4).

238. Heavenly Citizenship
	•	Synonyms: identity, belonging, status, eternal residence
	•	Lemmas: politeuma (G4175)
	•	Definition: Believers belong to God's eternal kingdom now. Our primary loyalty is to Christ's reign (Philippians 3:20; Hebrews 11:13–16).

239. Spiritual Maturity
	•	Synonyms: growth, fullness, completeness, Christlikeness
	•	Lemmas: teleios (G5046), tamîm (H8549)
	•	Definition: The process and goal of being fully formed in Christ. Requires time, teaching, testing, and Spirit-fueled transformation (Hebrews 5:14; Ephesians 4:13).

240. Fruitfulness
	•	Synonyms: productivity, spiritual impact, growth, multiplication
	•	Lemmas: karpos (G2590), peri (H6529)
	•	Definition: A life that bears visible outcomes of spiritual health—character, disciples, service (John 15:1–8; Galatians 5:22–23).

241. Anointing (Expanded)
	•	Synonyms: divine empowerment, spiritual seal, consecration
	•	Lemmas: chrismatos (G5548), māšaḥ (H4886)
	•	Definition: A spiritual endowment by the Holy Spirit for calling or ministry. Present in every believer, intensified for specific tasks (1 John 2:20, 27).

242. Watchfulness
	•	Synonyms: vigilance, alertness, readiness, discernment
	•	Lemmas: grēgoreō (G1127), šāmar (H8104)
	•	Definition: Remaining spiritually awake—especially in prayer and preparation for Christ’s return (Matthew 24:42; 1 Peter 5:8).

243. Martyrdom
	•	Synonyms: witness unto death, sacrifice, ultimate loyalty
	•	Lemmas: martys (G3144)
	•	Definition: Dying for one’s faith in Christ. The supreme earthly testimony to eternal allegiance (Acts 7:59–60; Revelation 6:9).

244. Reward
	•	Synonyms: inheritance, crown, recompense, blessing
	•	Lemmas: misthos (G3408), śākār (H7939)
	•	Definition: God's generous response to faithful service—not earned salvation, but an eternal gift of grace (Matthew 6:4; 1 Corinthians 3:14).

245. Spiritual Warfare (Expanded)
	•	Synonyms: battle, struggle, resistance, unseen conflict
	•	Lemmas: strateia (G4752), milchāmâ (H4421)

	•	Lemmas: nikaō (G3528), gābar (H1396)
	•	Definition: One who remains faithful through trial and temptation. Promised eternal reward and presence (Revelation 2–3; Romans 8:37).

247. Sanctified Identity
	•	Synonyms: new self, set-apart person, child of God
	•	Lemmas: hagios (G40), banîm (H1121 – sons)
	•	Definition: The believer’s new nature in Christ—holy, beloved, secure. Lived out in tension with the old self (2 Corinthians 5:17; Colossians 3:12).

248. Eternal Life (Expanded)

	•	Lemmas: zōē aiōnios (G2222 + G166)

249. Glory (Final)

	•	Lemmas: doxa (G1391), kābôd (H3519)
	•	Definition: The fullness of God's beauty revealed—and shared with believers. The goal of redemption and destiny of the saints (Romans 8:30; Revelation 21:23).

250. Amen

	•	Lemmas: amēn (G281), ʾāmēn (H543)
	•	Definition: A strong affirmation of truth. Used to seal prayers, promises, and doxologies. Christ is Himself called “the Amen” (Revelation 3:14; 2 Corinthians 1:20).
